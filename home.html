Cuba pre revolution

Cuba was a colonized country of spain. During their independence war america stepped in to help the cubans. The Americans then used this opportunity to strong arm Cuba into a neo-colonial state. Eventually leading to the dictatorship of batista, slavery, single crop economy and us gangster ran casino business. Real cubans were suffering.

During Revolution

Fidel Castro after years of planning and attempts to "legally" change the government to help his fellow cubans turned to armed revolution. His initial invasion army was small and was cut to a fraction after a disastrous landing of their boat The Grandma on the island. But there was still hope. Castro and Che Guevera survived the landing attack and were able to grow their force back up with the support of royal farmers and the large underground revolution network of revolutionaries.
The us of course did not like this as their were a lot of economic interest invested into cuba which they fear they would lose under a new government. The us attempted to assist batistas Military with funding, weapons, tanks plans and logistics. This in the end was not fruitful and Castro's revolutionary army eventually took control and batista fled after emptying out the government reserve of millions of dollars equivalent to billions today.. Not exactly setting up the new government for success.

Post revolution

This turn of events did not make several parties happy, including the us, slave owners and gangsters who lost their lucrative casino businesses. These three groups would and still do to this day try to destroy and undermine the new socialist government set up by castro. This includes but not limited to, cia funding to terrorist organizations, us military bombing, over 600 assassination attempts on castro and the longest lasting jab. The us blockade that has put a stranglehold on Cuba's economy for the last 60 years as it is still in Place. Which has cost their economy billions. Despite all this, Cuba has had some amazing accomplishments. Which include, ending homelessness, Eradication of illiteracy, universal healthcare, World renowned medical dr programs, assisting with other revolutions such as south africa, and so much more.
